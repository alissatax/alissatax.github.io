Minimal Object Poster

Minimal Object Poster / Fall 2020 / Intro to Graphic Design | instant camera poster

For this project I chose to create a poster celebrating the creation of the instant camera. I chose to go with a simple, retro feel. I also chose my color palette as a nod to the Polaroid brand which uses a rainbow color palette on their packaging. I have my final poster design as well as process work to show how I came to my final design.
