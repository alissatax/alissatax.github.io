Natural Beauty

Photo Series Final / Spring 2019 / Digital Photo | Natural Beauty

This series is called Natural Beauty. In this series, I am exploring the relationship between common beauty and hygiene products and plants. I’m exploring this relationship because many of these products contain unnatural materials or added chemicals. Through this series, I am showing that these products should be made with natural materials; such as plants. All of my photographs are taken close up, against a plain, simple background. I did this to allude to product photography that you would see when you’re shopping on a website or flipping through a beauty magazine. I also added a magenta tint to these photographs; my intention was to make them feel very dreamy and feminine. Together, my photographs form a catalog of products, but every photo tells its own story.
